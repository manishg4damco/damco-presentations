Security and Compliance
Enterprise-grade by design,
not by afterthought.
Every MCP deployment we build meets enterprise security standards from the ground up: integrated with your existing identity infrastructure, not layered on top of it.
🏛️
Data Never Leaves Your Systems
MCP is a query protocol, not a data migration protocol. Your data stays on your infrastructure, behind your perimeter. The AI queries through the MCP server and never circumvents it.
🔐
Inherits Your Existing RBAC and SSO
OAuth 2.1 authentication plugs directly into your existing identity provider: Okta, Azure AD, Ping Identity, via OIDC and SAML. No separate access model to configure or maintain.
📋
Full Audit Trail on Every Query
Every AI operation is logged: who asked, which tool was called, what data was accessed, and when. Satisfies GDPR, SOC 2, HIPAA, and SOX audit requirements without additional tooling.
🛡️
Cannot Exceed User Permissions
The AI operates within the requesting user's authorisation scope on every single query. It is architecturally impossible for the system to return data the user is not cleared to see.
Compliance Framework Alignment
MCP audit logging satisfies cross-framework requirements out of the box.
GDPR
SOC 2
ISO 27001
HIPAA
SOX
FedRAMP
On-premise deployment eliminates GDPR cross-border data transfer risk. Sensitivity label evaluation and per-operation scope controls are built into the MCP specification.
Why MCP is the Right Foundation
Released by Anthropic in November 2024 and donated to the Linux Foundation in December 2025, MCP is now a vendor-neutral open standard. Adopted by Microsoft, Google, OpenAI, AWS, Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Oracle within its first year. 97 million monthly SDK downloads and 5,800+ enterprise connectors confirm it as the standard for enterprise AI data connectivity.
