From Rules to Reasoning
What sets Agentic Automation apart from traditional workflow tools
Traditional Automation
Follows fixed rules
Breaks the moment reality deviates from the script
Single-function tasks
One trigger, one action. Cannot orchestrate multi-step processes
Requires human fallback
Every exception escalates to a human, defeating the purpose
Rule-coded per workflow
New business rules mean new development cycles
Agentic Automation
Reasons through context
Handles ambiguous situations by analyzing available data and choosing the best action
Multi-step, multi-agent pipelines
Orchestrates entire workflows across specialized agents working in parallel
Escalates only when needed
Handles standard cases autonomously; routes only genuine exceptions to humans
Configurable, not code-dependent
New business rules added through configuration, not development sprints
